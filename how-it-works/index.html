How it works

Oliver is here to make it easier.

01
Outreach

Connecting with You

With Oliver, addressing your outstanding balance is both simple and secure. We’re reaching out because one of our partners (ranging from banks and insurance firms to healthcare providers and telecom companies) has entrusted us with resolving an unsettled debt associated with your name or address. Leveraging intelligent technology, we determine the most suitable time and medium—be it email, text, WhatsApp, or traditional mail—to contact you.

02
Verification

Ensuring Accuracy

It’s essential to confirm the details of your outstanding balance and the entity to which you owe. We value the accuracy of information and strive to make it effortless for you to modify any discrepancies.

03
Incentives

Incentives for Prompt Action

Acting quickly on your outstanding balance can reap benefits at Oliver. We value your time and cooperation. In return, we’re ready to offer reductions on your balance if you’re able to provide us with the necessary information promptly, set up recurring payments, or clear the debt faster. This isn’t just about settling your financial obligations—it’s about rewarding your initiative and commitment.

04
Personalization

Tailoring Your Options

Just as no two snowflakes are identical, we understand everyone’s financial situation is unique. That’s why we offer a range of flexible payment options to find a solution that works best for you.

Pay Now: An efficient way to settle your debt, provided you’re comfortable with it.

Pay Later: Align the payment date with your payday within a 30-day timeframe, at no additional cost.

Pay in Installments: Break your payments into manageable chunks, with no interest or fees added.

Explore Other Options: We’re always open to discuss additional solutions that suit your circumstances.

05
Easy

Simplifying Solutions

We’ve streamlined the process of finding all the resources you need online.

By entering your unique 6-digit reference code via the link above, you’ll gain access to:

  • Your balance details
  • A comparison of different payment options and the ability to set up a plan
  • A live chat to report any issues
  • Support resources if you’re in a challenging situation
